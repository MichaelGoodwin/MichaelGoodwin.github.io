RPDeath

RPDeath is a FiveM script written in Lua that is used to enhance the role-playing experience of dead/hurt players. Enabling this script prevents automatic respawning and changes the respawn points to one of the 5 in-game hospitals.

When a player dies their body will stay in place and a 5 timer starts after which the player can respawn/revive themselves. During this time another player can respawn/revive them, which is commonly used for Paramedic role-playing.
